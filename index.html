The Code

Terms of Contract

  • Payment is non-refundable once a contract has been activated and the target has been engaged.
  • Identity of the client is never disclosed to the operator, the target, or any third party — before, during, or after the contract.
  • Contracts are fulfilled in order of priority tier and payment confirmation. High-Value contracts jump the queue.
  • We do not guarantee hospital time on targets who are already hospitalized, traveling, or in federal jail at time of contract activation.
  • We operate within Torn City's rules. This is an in-game service for the browser game Torn (torn.com). All transactions are in-game items and currency only.
  • Disputes are handled by Blacklist Recon leadership. Contact The Handler in-game for any contract concerns.
